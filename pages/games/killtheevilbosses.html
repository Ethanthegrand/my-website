Kill The Evil Bosses!!!


Play on Roblox!


Kill The Evil Bosses is my first game on this account and my first semi-successful game too. Released late in 2016, this game aimed to be a battle game, with 1 primary game mode, and 2 secondary game modes: "Boss Battle", "Zombie Survival" and "Free For All".




The Boss Battle gamemode featured a variety of bosses all their own attacks and difficult rating.

The Zombie Survival gamemode featured a game where the goal was to survive until the timer ends. This mode featured many types of zombies with different stats and weapons.
When the round starts, everyone is assigned as a survivor and must survive NPC and player zombies When a survivor dies, they become a zombie with all their gears replaced with a zombie tool.

The Free For All gamemode has all players be forced into the arena and to fight everyone to the death until one player remains or the timer runs out.

Beating ones of these gamemodes will have the game enter an intermission state. The large door in the arena will then open, leading to a simple obby which has a money chest at the end.


Around 2017-2018, the game would see a good amount of concurrent players. The game would typically reach Around 10-40 players at once during this time.
The game received it's last official content update in 2020. The game is now discontinued and only receives occasional bug fixes.

As of the 7th of October 2024, KTEB has over 126.9K visits.
